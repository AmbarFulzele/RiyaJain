19. 
If February 16th 2001 was a Saturday, then which day of the week was 1st January 2001?
A. Monday
B. Tuesday
C. Wednesday
D. Thursday
Status :  
Unanswered
Answer : B
The total number of days from 1st January, 2001 to 16th February, 2001 are (31+16)-1=46 days.
The number of odd days in 46 days = 46/7 = 6 weeks + 4 odd days.
If 16th February is a Saturday, then 1st January, 2001 is 4 days back from Saturday i.e., Tuesday.
Hence, 1st January, 2001 is a Tuesday.

21. 
On a particular day, if it is found that the clock is 8 minutes fast at 6:00 am and 4 minutes slow at 12 noon on the same day, then at what time did the clock show the correct time?
A. 8:00 am
B. 9:00 am
C. 10:00 am
D. 11:00 am
Status :  
Unanswered
Answer : C
The clock is uniformly losing time. At 6:00 am it is showing 6:08 am. At 12 noon, it is showing 11:54 am. So in a span of 6 hours, the clock is losing 12 minutes.
Therefore in 1 hour, the clock is losing 2 minutes. So to lose 8 minutes it takes 4 hrs.
So at 10:00 am, the clock had shown the correct time.
