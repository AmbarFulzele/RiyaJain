18. 
Which of the following years have the same calender as year 1788?
A. 1816
B. 1794
C. 1799
D. 1828
Status :  
Unanswered
Answer : D
The given year is a leap year. Hence, its calendar repeats after 28 or 40 years depending on if there is a non-leap century year as part of the calculation. In this case, a non-leap century year happens to be part of calculation. Hence, 1788 repeats after 40 years, i.e. 1828 and 1788 have the same calendar.

30. 
In which year is the number of Vernacular medium students, as a percentage of the number of English medium students, the greatest in Cochin?
A. 1996
B. 1998
C. 1999
D. 2000
Status :  
Unanswered
Answer : D
In Cochin, number of Vernacular medium students as a percentage of the number of English medium students in 1996 = 28/34, in 1998 = 40/46, in 1999 = 42/48, in 2000=47/53
In the above proper fractions, the difference between the respective numerator and its corresponding denominator is six. Hence the fraction having the greatest pair of integers as denominator & numerator will be greatest. Corresponding, the percentage in 2000 will be greatest.
